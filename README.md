Retail Performance Analytics – RCTTCE Prompting & Stakeholder Brief

Project Overview

This project demonstrates the use of the RCTTCE (Role, Context, Task, Format, Constraints, Examples) framework to design a structured AI prompt for business analytics. It also includes a generated Stakeholder Brief for a retail company experiencing declining performance across 30 stores over the last 18 months.

The goal is to show how structured prompting can support data-driven decision-making in a real-world business scenario.

 Problem Statement

A retail company with 30 stores is experiencing a decline in performance. The Analytics Team has been tasked with investigating potential causes using a dataset of approximately 50,000 records, including:

Store performance data
 Product sales information
  Customer transactions
  Loyalty programme activity
